Description

A salad is a dish consisting primarily of mixed pieces of raw or cooked vegetables, usually seasoned with oil, vinegar, or other dressing and sometimes accompanied by other ingredients such as cheese, fruit, meat, poultry, or fish.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Carrots
  • Salad dressing

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the vegetables.
  2. Combine the vegetables in a bowl.
  3. Add your favorite salad dressing.
  4. Toss gently and serve.
