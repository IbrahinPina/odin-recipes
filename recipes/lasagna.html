Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types of pasta. It is usually baked in layers with sauces and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese.

Traditionally, in Italy, lasagna consists of stacked layers of flat pasta alternated with fillings such as ragù (ground meats and tomato sauce), béchamel sauce, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef with onion and garlic.
  2. Stir in the tomato sauce and Italian seasoning.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta and some Parmesan.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  6. Bake until bubbly and golden brown.
